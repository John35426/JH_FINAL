Biggest whales in the oceans today

Blue whales are the largest animals to have ever lived on Earth. Their massive size and graceful movements make them an awe-inspiring sight.

Despite their enormous size, blue whales primarily feed on krill, tiny shrimp-like creatures. These whales consume tons of krill every day!

They produce the loudest sounds of any animal, using low-frequency calls that travel great distances underwater, making them one of the most vocal creatures in the ocean.

These majestic whales face numerous threats but continue to be a symbol of the power and beauty of the natural world. Efforts to conserve them are vital for their survival.
